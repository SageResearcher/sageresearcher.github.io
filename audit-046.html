Security Audit #046

Status: Evaluated | Target: Private Clinic Infrastructure

The Incident

Clinic management needed a full assessment of their public-facing digital footprint to comply with data protection standards.

The Diagnosis

Nmap service versioning revealed several outdated web services and an unpatched VoIP gateway exposed to the public internet.

The Hardening

Mapped the external attack surface, patched critical gateway firmware, and moved sensitive services behind a Zero-Trust proxy.

The Outcome

External exposure points reduced from 12 to 2; clinic systems successfully shielded from public-facing exploit attempts.

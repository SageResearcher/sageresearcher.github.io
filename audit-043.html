Security Audit #043

Status: Remediated | Target: Hospitality/Airbnb Client

The Incident

Client reported guests complaining of slow speeds and concerns over data privacy on a shared network across multiple units.

The Diagnosis

Identified several Rogue Access Points (APs) and a weak WPA2-PSK configuration vulnerable to KRACK attacks and unauthorized sniffing.

The Hardening

Transitioned the entire infrastructure to WPA3-Enterprise, implemented Client Isolation, and deployed a Rogue AP detection monitor.

The Outcome

Network security posture significantly improved; guest data isolation verified; unauthorized hardware detected and removed.

Security Audit #042

Status: Published | Category: Infrastructure Hardening

The Incident

Client reported persistent network lag and unauthorized devices appearing on the office Wi-Fi segment.

The Diagnosis

Using Nmap and Wireshark, I identified open SMB ports and a lack of WPA3 encryption, allowing for easy device spoofing.

The Hardening

I implemented a local firewall, disabled legacy SMB protocols, and configured a Zero-Trust access policy.

The Outcome

Unauthorized traffic eliminated; latency reduced by 40%.

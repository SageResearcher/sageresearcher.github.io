Security Audit #045

Status: Active | Target: Remote Professional Services

The Incident

Staff required secure access to the central database while working from various remote locations without the lag of traditional VPNs.

The Diagnosis

Existing OpenVPN setup was causing high latency (200ms+) and frequent packet loss, impacting productivity.

The Hardening

Deployed a Peer-to-Peer WireGuard VPN mesh. Configured ChaCha20 encryption for high-speed, low-overhead secure tunneling.

The Outcome

Latency reduced to sub-50ms; connection stability reached 99.9% uptime; secure remote access achieved without hardware bottlenecks.

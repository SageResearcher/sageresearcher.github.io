Security Audit #044

Status: Secured | Target: Logistics Firm

The Incident

Critical file servers were flagged for high vulnerability scores during a routine scan; risk of lateral movement via ransomware was high.

The Diagnosis

Found legacy SMB v1.0 enabled for old printer compatibility, leaving Port 445 open to EternalBlue-style exploits.

The Hardening

Disabled SMB v1.0 across all nodes, enforced SMB Signing, and implemented granular Port 445 filtering via the hardware firewall.

The Outcome

Internal vulnerability surface area reduced by 60%; critical infrastructure shielded from known worm-style attacks.
